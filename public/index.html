Place Badger

Placeholder images with badgers!

Place Badger provides an easy way to include placeholder images on your website. Just provide a width and a height in the url to Place Badger and it will generate an image for your layouts. Images include a header with the size of the image to help you identify images in your responsive designs.

Include a full color image like this: <img src='http://placebadger.org/600/200'> Or a grayscale image like this: <img src='http://placebadger.org/g/400/400'>
